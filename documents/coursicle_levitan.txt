CISC 3130 - Data Stuctures
Easily the best CS professor ive had so far. Her class is stress free while also being very knowledgable. She has a nice voice and explains things very clearly. TAKE HER!!!

CISC 3320 - Operating Systems
She is a decent professor. I took Operating Systems with her and all of her material was based off the zyBook. The whole class was basically her assigning zyBook chapters readings and their respective hws, and then her re-reading the same chapters in lectures. We had two exams and final. She didn't make the class difficult, so the class was ok.
